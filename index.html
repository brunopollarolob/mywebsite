Research & Internships

Thesis Project: Design and Implementation of the F-engine for CHARTS

My thesis work focuses on developing a full spectrometer system for the Canadian-Chilean Array for Radio Transient Studies (CHARTS) project. I'm developing a custom code for Xilinx AMD RFSoC 4x2 boards to process multiple antennas on a single digitizer through de-multiplexing.

The core innovation allows demodulating several antennas from a single analog-to-digital converter (ADC), enabling wide-bandwidth digitization of multiple 200 MHz bandwidth antennas. This development is central to CHARTS' digitization capabilities, as a single digitizer will be able to convert 32 antenna analog signals of the CHARTS' 256 elements.
